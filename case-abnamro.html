ABN AMRO

The ABN AMRO app proved itself to be the most convenient way for its users to handle their banking business.

Platform

App

Roles

Team

IceMobile

05.A

An introduction

Banking is serious business. And with over 6 million users, each new feature and improvement of the ABN AMRO app, has the potential to make tasks that used to be dull and repetitive, quick and delightful.
‍
After I just started working at IceMobile, a small team – myself included – took on the challenge to propose a redesign for the then outdated app. The proposal turned out successful, because soon after the client’s ‘GO’, we’ve assembled a full fletched team to build the redesign. I was tasked to lead the visual design direction.

05.B

A complete overhaul

When we started the redesign, ABN AMRO was in the midst of updating their branding, so strict guidelines where still missing.This offered a great opportunity to connect with the client’s designers and collaboratively create a new standard.
‍
One of the first screens that we rebuilt, is also the one our users see the most: the login. That’s why we wanted to do something interesting and created a login screen which felt more playful than before, but is also snappy enough to get to business as quick as possible.

05.C

Home, but better

We wanted the Dashboard to be the main road to all the user’s needs, so we carefully curated the content on this very important page.This is also the page that shows the user’s accounts and balances, so it had to be clear, simple and trustworthy.
‍
The design came together after multiple user tests and iterations, and hasn’t changed much over the years since the app has been live.
‍
All the love and effort we’ve put into the redesign was appreciated by the users, reflected by the great reviews.

05.D

Account

Your account is as dynamic as your life, so this needs to be reflected in your statements. We wanted to add personality to the account details, instead of showing plain strings of text.

05.E

Different ways to pay

Since ABN AMRO has such a large and diverse user base, their needs can be completely different as well.
‍
That’s why we explored a wide variety of ways to send money via the app: from straight forward transfers, to an implementation with the populair Dutch service ‘Tikkie’, and more experimental features.

05.F

Managed, by you

The app strives to be ‘the bank in your pocket’, so that also means being able to manage as many of your banking needs by yourself as possible.
‍
For our team, this resulted in the creation of an easy to use UI which enables you to change personal data, financial preferences and much more.
